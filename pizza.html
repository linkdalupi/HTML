Pizza

  1. roll out the pizza dough and preheat oven to 475 degrees
    2. - take out a pizza pan and roll out dough evenly. Add olive oil to lessen the stickiness. (should be about 1 inch thick) .
  2. Put on tomato sauce.
    3. - Take the tomato sauce and spread evenly on dough.
  3. Add cheese
    4. - grate cheese over the tomatoh sauce. Spread this evenly.
  4. Add toppings.
    5. - Anything you want! You can put pepperoni, mushrooms, olives, or leave it plain.
    - Put in the oven for half an hour. Check to make sure it does not get burned.
  5. Serve!
    6. - Serve hot or warm. Enjoy! :D
