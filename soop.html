Chicken Noodle Soup

  1. Sauté veggies
    2. - Add carrots to a large stock pot over medium-high heat. Sauté for 3 minutes. Add garlic and cook for another 30 seconds.
  2. Add chicken stock or broth.
    3. - Season the broth with salt (make sure to tate the broth before adding more salt)
  3. Add noodles and boil
    4. - Once the broth is boiling, add in the noodles. Cook just before the pasta is al dente
  4. Add chicken.
    5. - Add chiken meat. Taste the broth again, and add more seasoning if it is needed.
  5. Serve!
    6. - Serve hot or warm. add siders like bread if wanted. Enjoy! :D
